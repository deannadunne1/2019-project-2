Principles of Web Design

Utilize your knowledge of HTML and CSS to the best of its ability







Color

Determine the mood you want to set
Choose a palette of 1-4 colors and their corresponding shades that evoke this mood
Maintain high contrast between background and text colors

Create the Perfect Palette
Fonts & Text

Choose 1-2 fonts and use purposefully
Use text minimally to highlight key points
Ensure readability with large fonts and line-height
Use different font weights to contrast between header and body

Find a Font You Love

White Space

Use a minimalist design with plenty of whitespace in order to focus the readers attention on important information and give breathing room
